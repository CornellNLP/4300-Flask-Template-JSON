Funafuti is the capital of Tuvalu.

Get in[edit]

A valid passport and onward ticket is required for entry to Tuvalu.

By Plane[edit]

The only international airport in Tuvalu is located at Funafuti, with the amusing airport code of FUN.

Flights are carried out by Pacific Sun part of the Fiji Airways group using a ATR 42/72 air craft and Air Kiribati from Tarawa.

Get around[edit]

The best way to get around Funafuti is to hire a Motorbike/Scooter for AUD $10.00 daily. Most of attractions are located near the airport.

See[edit]

  • Mosque. Small but charming mosque is located near the airport.
  • Fetu'ao lima church. Lovely church with a very interesting interior
  • Tuvalu philatelistic bureau. You can see unique stamps from this island country
  • Funafala. Island with beautiful beaches
  • Avalau. Beautiful island of pristine beaches
  • Paava. Island covered by palm tree forest

Do[edit]

Funafuti airport's a good place for plane spotting. There're beautiful beaches in Northern end of the island. Riding a car/bike on the main island's road has an amazing view on the ocean. There're only small beaches you can stop on. There's also a beach near the airport. You can also watch the match on Vaiaku Stadium (near the airport).

Buy[edit]

The currency of Tuvalu is the Australian dollar (AUD), and the currency symbol is $. Tuvalu has also issued its own coins which are different from the Australian coins and can only be used on Tuvalu. The most common one is the Tuvalu 50 cent coin.

There is a handicraft centre at the Airport Terminal selling local handicrafts.

The Tuvalu Co-operative Society has 2 stores on Funafuti that sells a limited range of groceries.

Drink[edit]

  • Ocean Pub Bar. Bar and restaurant in Eastern end of the island.

Sleep[edit]

  • Vaiaku Lagi Hotel is the only hotel in Funafuti and is operated by the Tuvalu government. The hotel is located 50 metres from the Airport Terminal, facing the lagoon. The hotel has 16 rooms of a basic standard, together with a restaurant serving Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner. All rooms have air-conditioning and a telephone, although no hot water - only cold showers.

  • Vailuatai Lodge, Alapi, Funafuti, Tuvalu, ☎ +688-20664. [email protected] 60 - 80 AS$.
  • Militano Lodge, Senala, Funafuti, Tuvalu, ☎ +688-20497. Mobile phone: +688-91019 60 - 80 AS$.
  • Filamona Moonlight Lodge, Vaiaku, Funafuti, Tuvalu, ☎ +688-20833/20983. [email protected] Fax: +688-20951. Contact: Ms. Penieli Metia (Manager) 70 - 80 AS$.
